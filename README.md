## 📋 How to Add a Connection

If you work at any of these companies and are open to referrals, share the following with an admin:

- **Company Name**
- **Your Telegram Username** (e.g. `@yourname`)
- **Your Role / Department**
- **Willing to refer for:** (All roles / Specific roles only)
- **Any notes** (e.g. "Only for senior devs", "DM me first")

---

> ⚠️ **Reminder:** Connections are volunteers. Be respectful, do your research before reaching out, and don't spam. A referral is a favour, not a guarantee.
